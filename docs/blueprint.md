# **App Name**: AgroConnect SC

## Core Features:

- Homepage Banner: Rotating banner on the homepage to showcase high-quality images of fields, equipment, and products.
- Product Details: Detailed product pages with images, descriptions, specifications, downloadable files, and a 'Request a Quote' button.
- Interactive Map: Interactive map on the contact page powered by Google Maps.
- Contact Form: A contact form that sends the enquiry directly to Agro Saletense
- AI Product Recommendation: AI-powered tool suggesting related products and services based on the viewed product or service page, increasing user engagement and potential sales.
- Product and Service filters: Clear categorization of products and services.
- WhatsApp Button: Floating WhatsApp button for immediate contact with Agro Saletense.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A natural, muted green (#90EE90) evoking growth and health.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), providing a clean, neutral backdrop that highlights content and avoids stark contrasts.
- Accent color: Earthy orange (#D2691E) to draw attention to key actions and information, while reinforcing the brand’s connection to the land.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' (sans-serif) gives a modern, stylish, yet approachable feel to headings and titles. 
- Body font: 'Alegreya' (serif) ensures excellent readability for longer text sections. 
- Use icons to represent product categories, services, and contact methods.
- Clean, grid-based layout that is easy to navigate, emphasizing key information.
